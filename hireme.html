About Me

How I Got Here

I started making TTRPG content shortly after I was introduced to Dungeons and Dragons in 2017, and have since the developed my own simple TTRPGs and homebrew content for a variety of TTRPGs.

I created this website as a way to share my TTRPG content with the world, and to learn how to code a website from scratch. Though I learned a lot from university, there was still a lot to learn. This website was a way that I could combine both of my passions together and learn along the way.
